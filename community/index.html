FAQ

Where do I start?

Introduce yourself in Discussions, check open issues labeled “good first issue,” and read the contribution guide.

Do you fund external research?

We plan micro-grants and bounties tied to open tasks, evaluations, and safety studies.

How are decisions made?

We document proposals, pros/cons, and outcomes. Major decisions include community review and external input.
