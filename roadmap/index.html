A Transparent Path to AGI

We are building Ved AGI in the open, so the world can see progress and hold us accountable at every milestone.

Phase 1 – Foundation (2025)

  • Launch VedGov: leadership-focused LLM for healthcare and governance
  • Build scalable infrastructure and community hub
  • Release initial open-source research publications

Phase 2 – Expansion (2026)

  • Multi-modal models (text + vision + audio)
  • Enterprise-grade safety and interpretability features
  • Strategic partnerships with universities and research labs

Phase 3 – Acceleration (2027)

  • Deploy cross-domain reasoning systems
  • AGI agents for science, healthcare, and education
  • Grow developer ecosystem with APIs and SDKs

Phase 4 – AGI (2028–2030)

  • Fully autonomous, general-purpose intelligence
  • Aligned, human-supervised, globally accessible
  • Governance board and ethical oversight framework
