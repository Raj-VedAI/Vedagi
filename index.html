Mission

Deliver the world’s first open, safe, and human‑aligned AGI so advanced intelligence benefits all people — not just a privileged few.

  • Open: transparent research, reproducible results, and community collaboration.
  • Safe: robust evaluations, red‑teaming, and layered alignment techniques.
  • Human‑Aligned: oversight models and participatory governance.

Technology

  • Foundational Models: Transformer architectures with LoRA fine‑tuning and efficient quantization.
  • Alignment & Safety: RLHF, preference optimization, and constitutional AI with continuous evaluations.
  • Grounded Reasoning: Retrieval‑augmented generation and tool use for factual, auditable outputs.
  • Deployment: From community MVPs to modular, enterprise‑grade APIs.

Roadmap

  1. MVP (VedGov): leadership decision support for healthcare & governance.
  2. Harden Safety: evaluations, red‑team reports, and open alignment datasets.
  3. Scale Multi‑Domain: research collaborations and extensible tool ecosystems.
  4. Global Access: developer APIs and partner integrations.
