FAQ

Is Ved AGI open source?

We practice open science and release artifacts, code, and models whenever safe and responsible to do so.

How do you ensure safety?

Layered mitigations: preference-based training, red-teaming, interpretability tools, and governance with external oversight.

How can I contribute?

Join discussions, propose issues, and submit PRs on our GitHub. We also welcome research collaborations.
