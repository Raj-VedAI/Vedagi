Dual-Use Applications

MycelAI Conductor is engineered for both civilian environmental monitoring and defence/ISR operations — the same platform, deployed across radically different threat environments.

Agriculture Water Optimization

Sub-surface mycelium networks monitor soil moisture, nutrient flow, and root-zone health in real time — informing precision irrigation and reducing water usage by up to 40% without any wired infrastructure.

Permanent, tamper-evident biosensors embedded in forest and wetland ecosystems provide continuous, auditable carbon stock data — enabling verifiable carbon credits compliant with emerging Article 6 standards.

Defence / ISR Persistent Sensors

Drop-and-forget bio-sensors colonize terrain, surviving EMP, radiation, and extreme conditions where conventional electronics fail. MycelAI v1.1 includes defence-augmented features for persistent intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance in contested environments.
