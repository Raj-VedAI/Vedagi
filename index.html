Mission

Deliver the world’s first open, safe, and human‑aligned AGI so that advanced intelligence benefits all people — not just a privileged few.

Technology

  • Foundational Models: Transformer architectures with LoRA fine‑tuning and efficient quantization.
  • Safety & Alignment: RLHF and constitutional AI; rigorous evaluations; open oversight.
  • Knowledge Integration: Retrieval‑augmented generation and tool use for grounded reasoning.
  • Deployment: From community MVPs to modular, enterprise‑grade APIs.

Roadmap

  1. Launch MVP (VedGov) for healthcare & leadership use‑cases.
  2. Harden alignment, evaluations, and safety red‑teaming.
  3. Scale to multi‑domain AGI with open collaboration.
  4. Global access via developer APIs and partner integrations.
