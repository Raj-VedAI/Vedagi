The Problems We're Solving

Enterprise AI Hallucinations

Healthcare executives make decisions involving millions of dollars and patient outcomes. Financial institutions manage risks that could trigger regulatory penalties. These leaders can't use generic AI assistants — ChatGPT hallucinates numbers, Claude fabricates calculations. Current AI tools are built for consumer convenience, not enterprise accountability.

ClarityOps solves this by architecturally separating reasoning from calculation. All mathematics executes in sandboxed Python environments with complete audit trails. Hallucinations are impossible, not just improbable.

Blind Spots in Physical-World Intelligence

Agriculture, climate science, and national security all depend on monitoring environments that are too vast, too hostile, or too power-constrained for conventional sensor networks. A single crop field may span thousands of hectares; a denied-access theatre may lack any infrastructure at all.

MycelAI Conductor addresses this with radiation-hard, near-zero standby power, self-growing sensor colonies that establish themselves from spores — providing continuous underground and surface intelligence without logistics, batteries, or installation overhead.
