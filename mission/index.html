Principles

Openness

Build with transparency and share knowledge widely. Encourage scrutiny, replication, and global participation.

Safety

Prioritize robust evaluation, alignment research, and fail-safes before scale. Safety is continuous, not a checkbox.

Human Alignment

Center human values, dignity, and rights. Implement governance mechanisms with diverse stakeholder input.

Access for All

We’re designing systems that lower barriers to state-of-the-art intelligence for small teams and public institutions, not just Big Tech. Localized experiences and equitable access are key.

Accountability

Transparency reports, open evaluations, and independent advisory participation ensure that our mission is measurable and accountable.
